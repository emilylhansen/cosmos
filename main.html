0xABOUT

Hello, my name is Emily and welcome to my official self-built website (you can find the full code on my GitHub )! I am a computer science major with a triple minor in art history, dance, and visual arts at Boston University, but I am taking a year off to attend a coding bootcamp. I wasn't enjoying my classes and I wasn't learning what I wanted to learn in college, so I decided to put my formal education on hold and pursue my interest in web development. Following my completion of coding bootcamp I hope to find a full-time job as a web developer where I can continue my education through work experience.

I was introduced to web development during a freshmen year course, and I fell in love with the infinite programs I could build. I love to create and develop, and programming is a medium that I use for self expression, similar to how I use charcoal, watercolor, piano keys, and my body to construct forms, sounds, and movements. I need to use my mind to generate the unimaginable, and I want to bring those ideas to life as an engineer.
